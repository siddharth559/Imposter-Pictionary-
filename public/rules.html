Rulebook

Imposter Pictionary

A mobile-first drawing game for 4-8 players. One player is secretly the Imposter, everyone draws in turn, and the room tries to score points without revealing the secret word too easily.

Summary

Quick Rules

Players

Play with 4-8 players. The host chooses rounds, player cap, and optional custom words.

Turn

Each artist gets 60 seconds to draw. Everyone can see the timer and the current artist.

Guessing

Non-artists type guesses. Correct messages say the player predicted the word and never reveal it.

Imposter

The Imposter is secretly told their role. Their goal is to draw just badly enough to hide.

Accusing

After the first cycle, eligible players can press Accuse the Artist during the active turn.

Winning

Highest score at the end wins, but public scores may hide the Imposter until they are caught.

Setup

Creating a Room

  • The host enters a name, chooses drawing rounds, chooses the room player cap, and creates a room.
  • The host may paste a custom word corpus. Only 3-5 letter words are used.
  • If "Use only my words" is on, words come only from the submitted corpus.
  • If that toggle is off, submitted words are mixed with the built-in word pool to avoid repeats.
  • The game does not intentionally repeat words in a room.

Turn Flow

Drawing and Guessing

  1. The game randomizes turn order and secretly assigns one Imposter.
  2. The current artist receives the private word. Non-artists see only the hint boxes.
  3. The artist draws on the canvas for 60 seconds using brush, eraser, fill, color, and width tools.
  4. Other players guess in chat. The artist cannot guess.
  5. Each player can score from only their first correct guess for that turn.
  6. Players can vote for the next artist. If all eligible players vote, the turn advances early.

Scoring

Points

Guess Time Guesser Points
0-20 seconds 30
20-40 seconds 20
40-60 seconds 10
  • A normal artist gains 10 points for each correct guesser.
  • An Imposter artist gains 20 actual points for each failed guesser.
  • The public scoreboard hides the Imposter's real gain until the Imposter is correctly caught.

Hints

Word Hint Boxes

Every player sees boxes matching the word length. At 20 seconds, the first letter appears. At 40 seconds, the last letter appears.

Accusations

Accuse the Artist

  • Accusations are made only through the Accuse the Artist button, not by chat.
  • Accusations are locked for the first full cycle.
  • After that, eligible players may accuse only the current artist during the active 60-second turn.
  • After a correct accusation, everyone else's accuse button is disabled for the rest of that turn.
  • Correct accusation: the accuser steals floor(50% of the Imposter's actual score).
  • Wrong accusation: the accuser loses floor(50% of their own actual score).

Threshold

Minimum Score to Accuse

Threshold = 20 x (number of players - 1) x multiplier, rounded to the nearest 10.

  • 2 cycles: multiplier 0.75
  • 3-4 cycles: multiplier 1
  • 5+ cycles: multiplier 1.25

End Game

Finishing

The game ends after the selected number of cycles. The final screen shows scores and offers a new game option that returns players to a fresh lobby.
